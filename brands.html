perfomance

The perfect combination of BMW M high performance and the innovation of a plug-in hybrid. BMW M Hybrid, first electrified M high performance drive. High performance M TwinPower Turbo V8-cylinder petrol engine. Adaptive M suspension professional for high performance driving dynamics. M xDrive with M Sport differential. 480 kW (653 hp), torque 800 Nm. acceleration 0-100 km/h in 4.3s.

interior

Sitting in the new BMW iX feels like the future. A sophisticated and relaxed ambience is peppered with high-tech and luxurious comforts that simply redefine the joy of the journey. Re-engineered seats, including new lounge seats in the rear, feature a beautifully crafted asymmetrical diagonal design with additional contrasting seams. Other stylish elements include the transparent and intelligent centre console, which replaces buttons with sensors and features a iDrive Touch Controller in a high-quality glass optic.

exterior

The vehicle concept of the new XM embodies the primal essence of BMW M and brings it to life with powerful plug-in hybrid technology."For the very first time, an M vehicle comes to life with an M TwinPower Turbo V8-cylinder petrol engine with plug-in hybrid technology that generates a whopping 653 hp. Its extroverted design language impresses at an instant, while luxurious interior features, such as M lounge in the rear, vintage leather materials, and indirectly illuminated headliner, create an atmosphere of pure indulgence.

technology

Enjoy the luxury of choice and switch between the purely electric drive and the hybrid mode at the touch of a button. Thanks to the smooth interaction between the electric motor and powerful V8 engine, The XM combines the best of both worlds as its intelligent driving modes optimise the driving experience to your liking.

perfomance

Highly modern interior with slim instrument panel, BMW Curved Display and elegant interior features.

technology

the gladiator

perfomance

The Jeep Gladiator is a mid-size pickup truck that offers impressive performance both on and off the road. With its rugged build and powerful engine, the Gladiator is capable of handling almost any terrain.

interior

Under the hood, the Gladiator comes standard with a 3.6-liter V6 engine that delivers 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission. The Gladiator also offers an optional 3.0-liter EcoDiesel V6 engine that delivers 260 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque.

exterior

The Gladiator's performance is further enhanced by its off-road capabilities. It comes with a standard four-wheel drive system, high ground clearance, and skid plates to protect the undercarriage. The Rubicon trim level also includes front and rear locking differentials, an electronic sway bar disconnect, and rock rails for even more off-road capability.

technology

When it comes to towing, the Gladiator can tow up to 7,650 pounds, which is more than most other mid-size pickup trucks. It also has a payload capacity of up to 1,700 pounds, making it ideal for hauling heavy loads.
